Server Rules

🚫 No spawn camping

Respect player spawn areas. Camping near spawn is not allowed.

⏳ Follow the 1-hour grace period

No raiding or hostile actions within the first hour after server launch.

❌ No offline raiding

Raiding players’ bases when they are offline is prohibited.

👥 Team limit of 5

Teams are limited to a maximum of 5 players.

⚔️ Play fair and have fun

Cheating, exploiting, or hacking will result in a permanent ban.

💬 Respect others

Harassment, hate speech, or toxic behavior is not tolerated.
